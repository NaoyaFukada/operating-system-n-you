To change permissions:
  - chmod
  - Permission set:
    - u: owner
    - g: Group
    - o: Other users
  - To add or remove permissions, just use a plus or minus symbol that indicates who the permission effects.

To change permissions numerically:
  - The numerical equivalent of rws is:
    - 4 for read or r
    - 2 for write or w
    - 1 for execute or x
